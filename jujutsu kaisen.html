Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a manga and anime series that is written by Gege Akutami.
It is a story about how a high school student named Yuji Itadori runs into a world
filled with curses and sorcerers, and how his journey to defeat the king of curses.
The manga and anime series has many chracters with unique powers, and stories.
