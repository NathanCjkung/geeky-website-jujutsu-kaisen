Characters

The characters in Jujutsu Kaisen have unique heroes and villans, with their own stories and powers.

Heroes

The main heroes in the series are Yuji Itadori, Megumi Fushiguro, and Nobara Kugisagi
who are students at the Tokyo Jujutsu High School, with their teacher Satoru Gojo.
There are also other students in the Tokyo Jujutsu High School, such as Panda, Toge
Inumaki, and Maki Zenin. There is also another Jujutsu High School in Kyoto, with
students Mai Zenin, Mechamaru, Aoi Todo, Kasumi Miwa, and Momo Nishimiya, with their
teacher Utahime Iori.


Villains


The main vilains of the series is Sukuna, who is the king of curses, Mahito who is the
a cursed spirit birthed from human negative emotions, Geto Suguru who is a former jujutsu
sorcerer who turned into a curse user, Jogo who is a cursed spirit with the appearance
of a volcano, Hanami is a cursed spirit that looks like nature, and lastly Dagon who is
a cursed spirit that looks like a octopus.
