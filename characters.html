Characters

The characters in Jujutsu Kaisen have unique heroes and villans, with their own stories and powers.
The main heroes in the series are Yuji Itadori, Megumi Fushiguro, Nobara Kugisagi, and Satoru Gojo.
While, the main vilains of the series are Sukuna, Mahito, Geto, Jogo, and Hanami.
